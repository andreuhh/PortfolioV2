Tranqui

Jenuary 2020

Html, css, Jquery

Description

Second project realized during the mohole school course. The aim of the workshop was to create a blog that had the city of Milan as its theme. Andrea and I decided to focus on all those places in the city where you don't feel in the city. This was an opportunity to discover new bars, co-working and bookstores that we did not know before.



The site is divided into 3 sections: the first talks about places in the city, ideal to visit during the week. The second talks about the places outside the city where you can get in touch with nature, there are many, easily reachable by train or by car. There is also a third Lifestyle section with practical advice to stay fit and live our days better.
